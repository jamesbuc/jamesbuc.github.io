Welcome!

Welcome to Monumental Gaming DarkRP. To ensure that all our players have the best experience possible we have a comprehensive rule set that must be followed at all times. Please take some time to read through the rules and if you have any questions or need assistance with rule-breakers, please contact the staff team using admin chat e.g. @ help I'm being RDM'd.

Recent Changes

28 Jan - Rules updated to include many small rules which were being enforced but not acknowledged. Key changes include the reintroduction of kidnapping, new base standards for stores and thieves are now required to advert solo raids.

1. Don't be a minge.

  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Do not harass or bully other players.
  • Do not intentionally annoy people for your own amusement. This includes the use of high frequency/monotone sound emitters.
  • Do not put innocent lives at risk - nor act as a meat shield for others.
  • Do not Rope Spam (this includes making signs around the map with them).
  • Prop Spam is a permanent ban regardless of size, this is done to protect player progress.
  • When casting a demotion, discuss why in OOC first and give time for the person being demoted to respond e.g. // Demotion of Johnny McSmith for Failrp and NLR any objections?
    • Do not start a vote if objections are raised.
  • When using any donator commands (!gag, !mute) you must inform staff via admin chat (@) before doing so.

2. Roleplay (RP) properly.

  • Do not abuse /advert or /ooc, this means if you are being raided you cannot do "/advert COPS I'M BEING RAIDED HELP!". Use /cr to report a crime to them instead.
  • Do not AFK money farm, this means you cannot camp your base with printers and watch videos until you get raided.
  • Do not job abuse, this means you cannot change to a shop class, spawn shipments and change back to your previous job straight after.
  • Do not metagame e.g. if a hitman wants to see the Mayor you may not say "No way, you're a hitman and you have a hit on him."
  • Do not Random Death Match (RDM). You must have a valid RP reason (generally life-threatening) for shooting a person.
  • Follow your job description accordingly; don't make your job into something it's not designed to be.
  • Government jobs are not allowed to own Money Printers, unless it is stored in a bank.
  • If any transaction involving currency has occurred, the seller must provide said goods or a refund if sale is unavailable.
  • If custom laws have been added to the law board and the Mayor is killed, all laws on the law board must still be followed.
  • If you trespass into someone's property they reserve the right to kill you without warning, a public shop front would not be considered trespassing.
  • One handed and melee weapons are legal by default.
    • While one handed and melee weapons are legal, a cop can still arrest you for having the weapon drawn in public.
  • Mugging players is not allowed on this server. Thieves may pickpocket as an alternative.
  • Stealing during an RP transaction is not allowed, regardless of the job you are playing as.
  • Two handed weapons are illegal by default unless otherwise stated on the law board.

3. Apply roleplay concepts properly.

  • Follow Fear RP, you should value your RP life and not try to evade a person pointing a gun at your head.
    • If a player has a gun pointed at your head you may not draw a weapon on them.
    • When under FearRP you must follow the instigators orders. Getting killed for not following their orders is not considered RDM.
    • When initiating FearRP, please be reasonable with what you request of the victim. Ordering the victim to drop weapons or money is considered mugging.
    • When initiating FearRP, you must point your weapon at the victim for Fear RP to apply. If you turn around or stop threatening the victim at any point it will not be considered FailRP if he draws a gun and kills you.
    • FearRP does not apply to law enforcement roles or the terrorist.
  • Follow New Life Rule (NLR).
    • If you die, do not return to the place you were killed within 5 minutes. If you died on your property you may return - however only if there is not an ongoing raid. You must wait for any raids to complete before returning.
    • NLR also means that you must forget details about your past life. A police officer must not arrest the person that killed them, however if the player is wanted by the police, the officer may attempt to arrest.
  • No gangs or groups are at war by default. To start a war the gang or group leader must advert who the war is against and ask them to accept:
    • Gangs must have 2 members (including the leader) to commence a war.
    • If the opposing leader declines or doesn't reply you may not commence a war.
    • The Police/Government can not initiate or accept a war.
    • The war is over once the leader of either gang/group is killed.
    • You must /advert "War on X, accept?" where X is the gang or group you want to be at war with.
    • You must /advert "War over" once either leader has been killed.
    • You must wait 10 minutes between wars.
  • When kidnapping or being kidnapped you must abide by the following rules:
    • Kidnappings can only last up to 10 minutes. If no one pays the ransom you may kill or release the victim.
    • Only two thieves working together or a gang with two members (including the leader) may kidnap.
    • The maximum you can ransom a player for is $10,000.
    • You may not kidnap citizens or jobs that work alone.
    • You must advert kidnap before commencing a kidnapping and also advert after it has been completed e.g. /advert Kidnap or /advert Kidnap over.
    • You must follow Fear RP when being kidnapped.

4. Don't abuse any cheats or unintentional mechanics.

  • Bunny hopping is not allowed.
  • Do not use chairs to avoid death.
  • Money hacking, accepting/picking up hacked money or conspiring with money hackers will result in a permanent ban.
  • Props must be used only for building. Abusing props (by prop surfing or glitching) is not allowed.
  • The use of any cheats/hacks/scripts/macros will result in a permanent ban.
  • You are not allowed to manipulate the colours/materials of a player or world object.

5. Build fair and roleplay focused bases.

  • Custom vehicles are not allowed.
  • All fading doors must be lockpickable and have an attached keypad/button on both sides which is clearly visible.
    • Players should be able to find the keypad/button within 5 seconds of approaching the door.
    • The keypad must open for at least 4 seconds.
  • Any windows must be above waist height and below head height (not above), and must be open for at least a second. (Don't tap open/close windows)
  • Do not use constraints unless absolutely necessary (only weld when saving builds). Ensure you turn off all weld/no collide options in precision tool/stacker/easy weld.
  • Do not black box, this means making all the walls in your base completely black and making players unable to determine where they are.
  • Doors must be built with a single prop to ensure they can be lockpicked.
  • Fading doors are to be used as doors or windows only. Don't make bridges/staircases that fade.
  • Gun stores and other shops must be built within the following guidelines:
    • Stores must have a front counter area where trading with customers takes place. The shop owner must man the counter and also must be clearly visible to facilitate roleplay.
    • Stores without a front counter are not in the best interests of RP and will be removed.
    • The store owner must be in view at the front counter and be readily available to help customers.
    • The store itself should be primarily defended from inside the base. This means unmanned kiosks are not allowed.
  • No one-way visible props/materials/trickery is allowed. Both sides of a prop must be equally visible.
  • No-collided props to walk through is unrealistic and not allowed, even if it is transparent.
  • Only two doors may be used in a base. A single door is for the primary entrance, and a secondary door may be used to defend valuables.
  • Players that are building may place a sign disallowing people from raiding their base, only if:
    • They are currently building.
    • Base rules firmly apply when the base has valuables, or has locked doors and a raider is unable to see if it has valuables.
  • Players must not have to break glass in order to get into your base.
  • Signs are to be used for RP purposes only and may only be placed on or inside the owners property.
  • Tunnels must be wide enough for 2 people and must not force the raiders to crouch. World doors and fading doors are the only allowed "funnel" of one person at a time.
  • When pasting a save with advanced duplicator, you must ensure that it is pasted Frozen and Without Constraints.
  • You may not block key routes or build on roads or footpaths. This includes making toll booths for either roads or tunnels.
  • You may not use fences that can be shot through as apart of your base defences.
  • You may not enclose public resources or NPC's within personal property e.g. vending machines/drug dealer etc.
  • You may only purchase a door if you are using the interior of the building the door is attached to.

6. Be fair when raiding.

  • Do not steal items which are crucial for that persons job (e.g. the bomb generator or the microphone), they may be destroyed.
  • Do not kill bystanders unless they pose a threat to your raid.
  • If you die during a raid you can not come back after to keep helping with the raid.
  • Only raid the same store/base 15 minutes after your previous raid was finished.
  • Raids can only last up to 10 minutes, do not intentionally stall if you have finished the raid.
  • You may not heal yourself (includes health stations and health packs) or pick up additional armour during a raid.

4. Tools

Smart Constraint

  • Smart constraint an amazing tool when used properly. It allows you to easily select a lot of props and weld them together. It also produces a pretty pink colour when you click on a prop to show that you've selected it. You can select props by left clicking them. Note that some materials that have been applied on props may affect the distinctness of the pink colouration on selection, beware. Clearing all selected props is done by pressing the 'R' key or whatever your reload button is. Once the weld has been successfully completed, you should see a set of status messages in the bottom left of your screen.

Fading Door

  • Fading doors allows the player to easily get into his / her base without breaking the rules. Firstly you select a hotkey that will open the fading door VIA your keyboard and allow it to connect to a keypad and button.
  • Button to open the fading door, firstly you need to select the tool which is under construction and the third one down. This is where the hotkey comes in place, click the key to simulate box and change it to the hotkey that youve selected for the fading door. If you have none this correctly, using the use key, usually E, on the button, it should open the fading door.
  • Keypad to open the fading door, firstly you need to select the tool which is under construction, eleventh one from the top. This is also where the hotkey is needed, click the access granted box and change it to the hotkey that youve selected for the fading door. See below for more.

Keypad

  • Firstly you need to create a pass code for the keypad to work, it must be a 1-4 digit number e.g. 1234. Tick the secure mode and freeze, to ensure that no other players can see the code if they are near you and can see the keypad, and freeze, to make sure it will stick to the prop or wall. To change the how long the fading door will stay open, change the hold length to 4 seconds, which can be done by highlighting the current number and changing it to 4.

Weld

  • Players who don't have access to precision, can use weld to snap objects like precision move. You can achieve this by selecting weld tool and right clicking on a prop and right clicking it again on another prop, causing it to go into a rotation mode, and to complete it, simply right click again.

Precision Tool

  • Precision tool is an easier way to build bases, quickly and neatly. It allows you to snap props to the nearest 90?, push or pull props with exact accuracy, or move props from one prop to another. This can simply be done by selecting the amount that you wish push / pull amount, and hovering over the number currently there and replacing it with a number note going over 1000 will get you automatically banned.
  • Apply, selecting this will allow you to, Left click on a prop which will pull it, and right clicking on the prop will push the prop in the direction you are pointing at.

Advanced Duplicator

  • Advanced duplicator is the tool for saving your buildings after you've completed them. Make sure you smart constraint your props first before advanced duplicating.
  • To duplicate a set of props, all you need to do is right click one of the props, and the whole set will copy. After you get the 100% Ghosted completion message, all that's left to do is save the props to the server. In advanced duplicator, click the Save to Server button and type a name. That's it, your props are now saved! After you're done duplicating, please make sure to respawn your props. This means deleting the set of props go to Remove tool and right click a prop, it will delete any constrained props as well, and then place your duplication again.
  • To place a duplication, all you need to do is locate the dupe that you want to place in your advanced duplicator, click it so it turns blue, and left click, but please MAKE SURE you have the following options ticked.
    • Paste Frozen
    • Paste w/o Constraints
    • These options respawn the props without constraints in order to stop the server from lagging.

Stacker

The options at the bottom allow you to change how far away it will spawn from the base prop, or what angle it will spawn at. Left clicking will spawn in the prop.

Wire Hydraulics

  • Normal hydraulics were banned due to miss use by players, this tutorial will show you create wire hydraulics correctly. What you will need is:
    • The props you are using the hydraulics on
    • Wire Hydraulic
    • Smoother Gate > Search gate, then smoother in the gate tool
    • If than else > Search gate, then smoother in the gate tool
    • Constant Value
    • Wire Button
  • To wire it up:
    • Firstly, you need to set up the hydraulic to the two objects ( in this case, we are making an elevator ) ensuring that fixed is ticked before you do this.
    • Right click on the base prop ( the prop that will be on the ground ) so the rope is showing.
    • Left click anywhere ( preferably on the ground, or where you are putting all your gates ).
    • Next physgun the prop that the player will stand on to go up, to the desired height you want.
    • Next enter the following values in to the constant value, hover over the hydraulic to reveal a value that shows how high the prop is and enter that value into the constant value.
    • Add one which is what the elevator will decent to, add 100000, 70 and how long you want the elevator to the hold on the ground for ( in this case 7 ).
    • Wire A of the smoother to out on the If then else gate, and rate of the smoother to 70 on the constant value.
    • Moving on to the delay gate, wire CLK to the button, wire hold on the delay gate to 7 on the constant value.
    • Wire A on the If than else gate to delay gate out, B to 1 on the constant value and C to the height of the hydraulic.
    • Length on the hydraulic to smoother out, and constant on the hydraulic to 100000 on the constant value.

Wire Camera

  • Wire camera is basically the same as the RT camera, except lots of people use the RT cameras at once. Using this will avoid arguments, and you can have a personal RT camera. What you will need:
    • Camera Controller
    • Numpad Input ( set it to toggle and remember the key you set )
  • Firstly wire the Position vector of the Camera to the Vector/Position of the GPS. And wire Activated of the Camera to the numpad Input. Press the key for the numpad input and it will immediately show you what your camera is seeing. What you see is not the point of view from the camera, but from the GPS. To change the location of your 'camera' you need to move the GPS. You can rotate your screen when inside the camera.
